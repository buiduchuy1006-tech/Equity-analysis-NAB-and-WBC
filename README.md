# Comparative Equity Analysis (NAB vs WBC)

## About the Project

Have you ever once questioned yourself:"If I had enough money to buy 2000 shares from a company, which one should I invest in?"? I guess that is definitely not an easy decision. In fact, an investment decision should be made based on numerous analysis regarding the Return on Investment, the level of risk, etc. Therefore, I would like to conduct this project to simulate a simple scenario where we analyse the performances of two equities based on what happened in the past.

## Methodology
In this project, I used historic share's price data from 2021 to 2026 of the National Australia Bank (NAB) and that of Westpac Banking Corporation (WBC), sourced from TradingView. Given that, I have developed a simple dashboard with interactive charts representing net profit, total value of share, price trajectories and the ROI. Thereby, I assessed the investment performances with some insights and evaluate the equities' future potential.
